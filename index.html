Instructions

Click on a tile to flip it over, revealing a cute cat. Click another tile to flip another. If the images match, they will remain flipped over. If they don't match, they will flip face-down again. Try use your memory to match all the tiles before the timer runs out! Be careful of how many tiles you choose, if you use 16 guesses, you lose the game!
